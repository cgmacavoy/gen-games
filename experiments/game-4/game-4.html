Instructions

You will explore a new world called Critter Country. You are part of a team of turkers and you are tasked with learning about the species in this world and teaching others about them.

You have a special device (a CritterDex) that can detect the critter you see and tell you what it's called. After exploring the world, you will be tested to see what you've learned about the critters.

Then, you will teach another turker about the critters. You will do this by writing a message to them, and we will send the message to them on your behalf. The turker who receives your message will have to identify the critters but won't have the CritterDex.
