Instructions

You will explore a new world called Critter Country, where scientists have discovered several new species.

You will try to learn about one critter at a time. For example, you might start, by learning about the "wudsy". For each species, you will be given 25 sets of critters. For each set, you will have to determine which of the displayed creatures belong to the speices and which do not. After you label the creatures, the correct labels will be displayed and will remain on screen for futher reference. After the 25 sets of labeling a specific species, e.g. "wudsy", you will repeat this identification game for two additional species.

Please note that the species are completely independent of one another. A creature that was previously categorized as a "wudsy" may be categorized as another species, when learning about a different species. The definition of each species is completely independent of one another.

Please note, if you incorrectly label a critter within a set, you will have to wait 5 seconds before the next set of critters.

The wudsy objects in each set are surrounded by a square.

Given the above examples, which of the objects in this set are wudsy?
