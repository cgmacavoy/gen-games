Instructions

You will explore a new world called Critter Country, where scientists have discovered several new species. You will be shown critters, one at a time and will be asked whether the critter belongs to the "wudsy" species. We will keep a log of all the critters you see and box the ones that were "wudsy" for your reference.

Please note, if you incorrectly label a critter, you will have to wait 5 seconds before the next one.
