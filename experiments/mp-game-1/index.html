Instructions

You will explore a new world called Critter Country. You are part of a group of turkers and you are tasked with learning about the species in this world and teaching others about them.

After exploring the world, you will be asked to teach another turker about the world. You will do this by writing a message to them, and we will send the message on your behalf. The turker who receives your message will have to move through the world, interact with critters, and complete various tasks.

What you learn in this task will be helpful to them. At the end of the experiment, you will send a message to the next turker.
